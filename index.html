About Me

Hello! Welcome to my Portfolio. I went to Penn State University where I received a Bachelors in Recreation, Parks, and Tourism Management, and moved to Colorado to begin my life soon after. Since moving to Colorado a decade ago, I've been working in management and sales. When the pandemic occurred and turned everything upside down, I started to look into Software Development.


After a lot of thought in the matter, I decided to take a coding bootcamp and get that path started. I enrolled in a Full Stack Certification Course through Denver University and not only was I able to successfully navigate the course and complete the certification, but I quickly fell in love with coding. While I've learned enough to know I have a lot more to learn, I'm very excited about this path I've begun and am excited to see where it leads me next.
